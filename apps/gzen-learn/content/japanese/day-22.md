## Grammar Pattern

**〜に際して / 〜に際し** — "On the occasion of / at the time of"

A very formal expression used for important or ceremonial occasions. Used to mark a specific timing for an action or advice.

**Structure:** Noun / Verb (dictionary) + に際して + action/advice

> 💡 Similar to 〜にあたって but に際して tends to be used for more formal, ceremony-like situations.
