## Exam Strategy for JLPT N1

### 📝 Section 1: Language Knowledge (Vocabulary/Grammar)
- **Time**: ~110 minutes total
- Grammar questions often test **register** (formal vs informal) and **nuance**
- Watch for: かねない vs かねる, ざるを得ない (する → せざるを得ない), ものの vs けれども

### 📖 Section 2: Reading
- **Tip**: Read the questions FIRST, then the passage
- N1 passages use: formal register, abstract nouns, complex sentence structures
- Key vocabulary in academic writing: 〜に基づいて、〜を踏まえ、〜をめぐって、〜に際し

### 👂 Section 3: Listening
- Note-taking is allowed — jot down key numbers, names, and conditions
- Focus on the QUESTION being asked, not every word
- Watch for: discourse markers (しかし、一方、つまり、したがって)
