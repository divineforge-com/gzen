## Grammar Pattern

**〜ものの** — "Although / Even though / But"

The speaker acknowledges the first clause as true, but then presents a contrasting or unexpected reality in the second clause.

**Structure:** Verb / Adj (plain) + ものの + contrasting result

> 💡 Similar to 〜けれども but more formal and written. The contrast is often a disappointment or problem.

## Example Sentences

1. <ruby>日本語<rt>にほんご</rt></ruby>の<ruby>勉強<rt>べんきょう</rt></ruby>を<ruby>始<rt>はじ</rt></ruby>めたものの、なかなか<ruby>上達<rt>じょうたつ</rt></ruby>しない。
   *Although I started studying Japanese, I'm not making much progress.*
　*（繁中：雖然開始學日語，但進步不大。）*

2. <ruby>合格<rt>ごうかく</rt></ruby>はしたものの、<ruby>自信<rt>じしん</rt></ruby>がない。
   *Although I passed, I don't feel confident.*
　*（繁中：雖然通過了，但缺乏自信。）*

3. <ruby>大学<rt>だいがく</rt></ruby>を<ruby>卒業<rt>そつぎょう</rt></ruby>したものの、<ruby>就職<rt>しゅうしょく</rt></ruby>できていない。
   *Although I graduated from university, I haven't been able to find a job.*
　*（繁中：雖然大學畢業了，但還沒找到工作。）*
