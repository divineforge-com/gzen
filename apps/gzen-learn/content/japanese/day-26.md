## Grammar Pattern

**〜ならいざ知らず** — "I don't know about X (extreme case), but (in normal circumstances)…"

The speaker presents an exceptional/extreme case (X) as perhaps understandable, but then says the current situation is clearly different and unacceptable.

**Structure:** Noun + ならいざ知らず + main statement (negative/criticism)
