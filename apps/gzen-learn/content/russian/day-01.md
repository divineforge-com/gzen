## Welcome to Russian!

Russian uses the **Cyrillic** alphabet — 33 letters total. Many look like Latin letters, some look familiar but sound different, and some are completely new.

## Letters That Look Familiar But Are Different

> ⚠️ Watch out! These Cyrillic letters look like Latin but have **different** sounds:

| Looks like | Actually sounds like |
|-----------|---------------------|
| **Р** | R (not P!) |
| **С** | S (not C!) |
| **Н** | N (not H!) |
| **В** | V (not B!) |
| **Х** | KH (like Scottish "loch") |
