## Prepositional Case (Предложный падеж)

The prepositional case is **only used after prepositions** — mainly:
- **в / на** (in / at — location)
- **о / об** (about)
- **при** (under the rule of / in the presence of)

Answers **о ком? о чём?** (about whom? about what?)

## Prepositional Endings

| Gender | Nominative | Pronunciation | Prepositional | Pronunciation |
|--------|-----------|--------------|--------------|--------------|
| Masculine | стол | stol | стол**е** | sta-LYE |
| Masculine (-й) | музей | moo-ZYEY | музе**е** | moo-ZYE-ye |
| Feminine (-а) | книга | KNEEga | книг**е** | KNEEgye |
| Feminine (-я) | земля | zyem-LYA | земл**е** | zyem-LYE |
| Neuter | окно | ak-NO | окн**е** | ak-NYE |

> Special: some masculine nouns take **-у**: лес → в лесу, сад → в саду, год → в году

## Location: в vs на

| Use **в** | Use **на** |
|-----------|-----------|
| в школе *(v SHKO-lye)* (at school) | на работе *(na ra-BO-tye)* (at work) |
| в городе *(v GO-ra-dye)* (in the city) | на улице *(na OO-li-tse)* (on the street) |
| в России *(v raSSEEyi)* (in Russia) | на севере *(na SYE-vye-rye)* (in the north) |
| в магазине *(v ma-ga-ZEE-nye)* (at the store) | на концерте *(na kan-TSYER-tye)* (at a concert) |

## Examples

- Я живу **в Москве**. *(ya zhi-VOO v maSKVYE)* *(I live in Moscow.)*
- Книга **о любви**. *(KNEEga a lyoo-BVI)* *(A book about love.)*
- Мы говорим **о погоде**. *(my ga-va-RIM a pa-GO-dye)* *(We are talking about the weather.)*
