## Adjective Agreement

Russian adjectives **agree** with the noun in gender, number, and case.

| Gender | Ending | Example |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Masculine | -ый / -ий | красный дом (KRASniydом) (red house) |
| Feminine | -ая / -яя | красная машина (KRASna-ya ma-SHEE-na) (red car) |
| Neuter | -ое / -ее | красное яблоко (KRASna-ye YABla-ka) (red apple) |
| Plural | -ые / -ие | красные цветы (KRASni-ye tsve-TY) (red flowers) |

## Basic Adjectives

| Russian | Pronunciation | Meaning | Opposite |
|---------|--------------|---------|---------|
| большой | bal'-SHOY | big | маленький (MA-len'-kiy) (small) |
| хороший | kha-RO-shiy | good | плохой (pla-KHOY) (bad) |
| новый | NO-viy | new | старый (STA-riy) (old) |
| красивый | kra-SEE-viy | beautiful | некрасивый (nye-kra-SEE-viy) |
| интересный | in-tye-RYES-niy | interesting | скучный (SKOOCH-niy) (boring) |
| дорогой | da-ra-GOY | expensive | дешёвый (dye-SHYO-viy) (cheap) |
