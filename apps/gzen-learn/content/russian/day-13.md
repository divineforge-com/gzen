## Instrumental Case (Творительный падеж)

Answers **кем? чем?** (by whom? by what?)

Used for:
1. **Instrument/means**: writing WITH a pen
2. **Accompaniment**: going WITH someone
3. **Profession** (with быть): I am a teacher
4. **After prepositions**: с (with), за (behind/for), перед (in front of), между (between), над (above), под (under)

## Instrumental Endings

| Gender | Nominative | Pronunciation | Instrumental | Pronunciation |
|--------|-----------|--------------|-------------|--------------|
| Masculine | студент | stoo-DYENT | студент**ом** | stoo-DYEN-tom |
| Masculine (-й) | музей | moo-ZYEY | музе**ем** | moo-ZYE-yem |
| Feminine (-а) | книга | KNEEga | книг**ой** | KNEEgoy |
| Feminine (-я) | земля | zyem-LYA | земл**ёй** | zyem-LYOY |
| Neuter | окно | ak-NO | окн**ом** | ak-NOM |

## Key Patterns

**Means/instrument:**
- писать **ручкой** *(pi-SAT' ROOCHkoy)* *(to write with a pen)*
- ехать **автобусом** *(YE-khat' af-TO-boo-som)* *(to go by bus)*

**Accompaniment (с + instrumental):**
- Я иду **с другом**. *(ya i-DOO s DROOgom)* *(I'm going with a friend.)*
- Кофе **с молоком**. *(KO-fye s ma-la-KOM)* *(Coffee with milk.)*

**Profession (быть + instrumental in non-present tenses):**
- Он был **учителем**. *(on byl oo-CHI-tye-lyem)* *(He was a teacher.)*
- Она станет **врачом**. *(a-NA STA-nyet vra-CHOM)* *(She will become a doctor.)*
