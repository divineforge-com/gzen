## Genitive Case (Родительный падеж)

The genitive answers **кого? чего?** (of whom? of what?) and is used for:
1. **Possession** (of / 's)
2. **Absence** with нет (there is no)
3. **After numbers** 2–4 (genitive singular), 5+ (genitive plural)
4. **After some prepositions**: из, от, до, без, для, у, около

## Genitive Endings

| Gender | Nominative | Pronunciation | Genitive | Pronunciation |
|--------|-----------|--------------|---------|--------------|
| Masculine | стол | stol | стол**а** | sto-LA |
| Masculine (-й) | музей | moo-ZYEY | музе**я** | moo-ZYE-ya |
| Feminine (-а) | книга | KNEEga | книг**и** | KNEEgi |
| Feminine (-я) | земля | zyem-LYA | земл**и** | zyem-LI |
| Neuter (-о) | окно | ak-NO | окн**а** | ak-NA |

## Key Uses

**Possession:**
- книга **студента** *(KNEEga stoo-DYEN-ta)* *(the student's book)*
- столица **России** *(sta-LEE-tsa raSSEEyi)* *(the capital of Russia)*

**Absence (нет + genitive):**
- У меня нет **книги**. *(oo me-NYA nyet KNEEgi)* *(I don't have a book.)*
- Здесь нет **воды**. *(zdyes' nyet va-DY)* *(There is no water here.)*

**У + genitive = "has":**
- **У меня** есть книга. *(oo me-NYA yest' KNEEga)* *(I have a book.)* — literally "by me there is a book"
- **У неё** есть машина. *(oo nye-YO yest' ma-SHEE-na)* *(She has a car.)*
