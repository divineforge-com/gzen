## Dative Case (Дательный падеж)

Answers **кому? чему?** (to whom? to what?)

Used for:
1. **Indirect object** (giving something TO someone)
2. **Age expressions**: Мне … лет (I am … years old)
3. **Feelings/states**: Мне холодно (I am cold)
4. **After prepositions**: к (towards), по (along/by)

## Dative Endings

| Gender | Nominative | Pronunciation | Dative | Pronunciation |
|--------|-----------|--------------|--------|--------------|
| Masculine | студент | stoo-DYENT | студент**у** | stoo-DYEN-too |
| Masculine (-й) | музей | moo-ZYEY | музе**ю** | moo-ZYE-yoo |
| Feminine (-а) | книга | KNEEga | книг**е** | KNEEgye |
| Feminine (-я) | земля | zyem-LYA | земл**е** | zyem-LYE |
| Neuter | окно | ak-NO | окн**у** | ak-NOO |

## Key Patterns

**Indirect object:**
- Я дал книгу **студенту**. *(ya dal KNEEgoo stoo-DYEN-too)* *(I gave the book to the student.)*

**Age:**
- **Мне** двадцать лет. *(mnye DVAtsat' lyet)* *(I am 20 years old.)*
- **Ей** тридцать лет. *(yey TREEtsat' lyet)* *(She is 30 years old.)*

**Feelings/states (dative of person):**
- **Мне** холодно. *(mnye KHOladna)* *(I am cold.)*
- **Тебе** интересно? *(tye-BYE in-tye-RYES-na?)* *(Are you interested?)*
- **Ему** скучно. *(ye-MOO SKOOCHna)* *(He is bored.)*
