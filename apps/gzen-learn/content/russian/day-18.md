## Full Adjective Endings Table

Using **новый** (new) as the model:

| Case | Masculine | Pronunciation | Feminine | Neuter | Plural |
|------|----------|--------------|---------|--------|--------|
| Nominative | новый | NO-viy | новая (NO-va-ya) | новое (NO-va-ye) | новые (NO-vi-ye) |
| Accusative (inanim.) | новый | NO-viy | новую (NO-voo-yu) | новое (NO-va-ye) | новые (NO-vi-ye) |
| Accusative (anim.) | нового | NO-va-va | новую (NO-voo-yu) | — | новых (NO-vikh) |
| Genitive | нового | NO-va-va | новой (NO-voy) | нового (NO-va-va) | новых (NO-vikh) |
| Dative | новому | NO-va-moo | новой (NO-voy) | новому (NO-va-moo) | новым (NO-vim) |
| Instrumental | новым | NO-vim | новой (NO-voy) | новым (NO-vim) | новыми (NO-vi-mi) |
| Prepositional | новом | NO-vom | новой (NO-voy) | новом (NO-vom) | новых (NO-vikh) |

## Soft Adjectives (-ий ending)

Some adjectives use soft endings. Model: **синий** (SEE-niy) (blue/dark):
- синий (SEE-niy) (m) / синяя (SEE-nya-ya) (f) / синее (SEE-nye-ye) (n) / синие (SEE-ni-ye) (pl)

## Examples in Context

- **Новый** дом большой. *(NO-viy dom bal'-SHOY)* *(The new house is big.)* — nom. masc.
- Я вижу **новый** дом. *(ya VEE-zhoo NO-viy dom)* *(I see the new house.)* — acc. inanim. masc.
- Я живу в **новом** доме. *(ya zhi-VOO v NO-vom DO-mye)* *(I live in the new house.)* — prep. masc.
- Книга **новой** студентки. *(KNEEga NO-voy stoo-DYENT-ki)* *(The new student's book.)* — gen. fem.

## Adjective-Noun Agreement Rule

The adjective ending must match the noun in:
1. **Gender** (masculine/feminine/neuter)
2. **Number** (singular/plural)
3. **Case** (nominative/accusative/genitive/dative/instrumental/prepositional)
