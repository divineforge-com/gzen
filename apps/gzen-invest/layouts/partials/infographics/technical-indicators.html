Indicator Setup / Formula 📈 Bullish Signal 📉 Bearish Signal 💡 Pro Tip
🔴 RSI
Relative Strength Index
 Range: 0–100 · Period: 14
RSI = 100 − 100/(1+RS) | RS = AvgGain/AvgLoss 		RSI < 30 → Oversold (potential buy) RSI > 70 → Overbought (potential sell) Divergence = early reversal signal
📘 MACD
Moving Avg Convergence Divergence
 MACD = 12EMA − 26EMA | Signal = 9EMA
Histogram = MACD − Signal line 		MACD crosses above Signal → Bullish MACD crosses below Signal → Bearish Zero-line crossover confirms trend
📉 Moving Averages
SMA / EMA
 SMA — Simple · EMA — Exponential (recent bias)
Key levels: 20 · 50 · 100 · 200 periods 		Golden Cross: 50MA crosses above 200MA Death Cross: 50MA crosses below 200MA MA = dynamic support/resistance
📊 Volume Analysis
OBV / Volume bars
 Volume confirms price moves
OBV — On-Balance Volume indicator 		Price ↑ + Volume ↑ = Strong bull move Price ↑ + Volume ↓ = Weak move (caution) Volume spike = institutional activity
📐 Bollinger Bands
BB (20, 2)
 Upper = 20SMA + 2× Std Dev
Lower = 20SMA − 2× Std Dev 		Price at lower band = potential buy Band squeeze = breakout incoming! 95% of prices stay within bands
🌀 Fibonacci Retracement
Fib Levels
 Key levels: 23.6% · 38.2% · 50% · 61.8%
Draw from swing low → swing high 		Price bounces at fib level = buy zone Break below 61.8% = trend reversal 61.8% = Golden Ratio (strongest)

🔗 Combine 2–3 indicators for confirmation · No single indicator is 100% accurate
